Hunting

Wisconsin has a lot of wildlife to hunt. The state requires anyboybody born after January 1st, 1976 to complete and pass a hunter safety class. The education provided in a hunter safety class is important. It teaches important rules and how to handle your rifle, shotgun, bow and arrow, and other guns. The class and rules are here to protect you and the people around you. Please read the yearly rules and regulations! You never know when a zone will change. This will prevent you from getting fined and losing your hunting privileges.
